MANDATORY STRUCTURAL RULES
- No empty desktop space
- Every split section body must be dense enough to visually match its paired media

- No floating CTAs
- Buttons must align across rows where possible
- Equal-height cards are mandatory
- Major sections must feel visually complete, never half-empty
